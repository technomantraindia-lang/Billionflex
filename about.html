Our Journey

Our History

Decades of pioneering innovations and excellence in the printing and packaging industry.

1970
First and the only company to manufacture plastic bags in Baroda, India.
1974
Contacted by Government of India to manufacture customized bag sheets for forest department.
1986
Billion Flex (India) Pvt. Ltd. enters the market.
1987
Multicolor carry bags with upto 12 colors printing introduced in India for the first time.
1992
Multicolor carry bags with centrally aligned printing in side gusset for the first time in India.
1994
Enters the global markets of Middle East, Africa, Europe.
1995
Introduces modern handles for carry bags like European soft loop handle, reinforced patch handle and variety of die cut handles.
1996
Introduces in-house specialized box type carry bags with cord, rivettes, and cardboard reinforcements.
1997
Sets up an advanced Shrink Sleeves manufacturing in house facility.
2001
Sets up highly sophisticated 8 color rot gravure printing press.
2003
Sets up computerized injection molding machines for making of rigid handles in-house.
2004
Enter the global market of North America.
2009
Inauguration of Global office in United States.
2010
Acquired and installed state-of-art 9-color and 10-color roto gravure printing presses.
2015
Launch Tamper proof Courier Bags/E-commerce Bags as a new products.
